Ramen recipe:

Photo by Ramaz Bluashvili (the image is of a different ramen)

This recipe is sadly completely made up and not tested because im not going to use someone elses recipe. I dont want to get sued or something.

Ingredients

  • 2 portions - Ramen noodles
  • 2 cloves - Garlic
  • 1 tbsp - Soy sauce
  • Your choice - Spring onion
  • 2 - Eggs
  • 2 portions - Broth
  • A good amount - any Meat

Directions

Step 1: Cook the noodles.

Step 2: Boil broth and add garlick and soy

Step 3: Add eggs and boil

Step 4: Chop spring onion

Step 5: Fry meat.

Step 6: Put everything in a bowl.

Now you have a ramen, it is possibly tasty.
