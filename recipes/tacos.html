Taco recipe:

Photo by Sergio Arreola (the image is not from this recipe)

This recipe is sadly completely made up and not tested because im not going to use someone elses recipe. I dont want to get sued or something.

Ingredients

  • 4 - Taco shells
  • 2 cloves - Garlic
  • 1/2 Can - Salsa
  • 1-2 - Red onion
  • 1 handful - parsley
  • A good amount - Ground beef

Directions

Step 1: Fry the meat

Step 2: add garlic to meat

Step 3: Chop onions and parsley

Step 4: Put everything in the taco shells

Now you have tacos, they are possibly tasty.
